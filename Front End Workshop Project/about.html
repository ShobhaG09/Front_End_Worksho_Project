About me

work at the intersection of

talent, business and social impact

Our new-age learning solutions, powered by best-in-class technology, deliver measurable business and individual impact. Centum's expertise helps organizations drive employee productivity, develop a pipeline of future leaders, and craft customized learning journeys. Our work also involves integrating livelihood skilling and capability development into an organisation's CSR strategy. ...
Across industries. Across continents Centum's expertise has grown organically over the past 15 years as the scope and scale of our work increased exponentially. We serve leading brands and companies in 15+ countries (India and Africa) across diverse industries like Consumer Internet, Auto, BFSI, Retail, Consumer Durable, FMCG and Telecom to name a few. Over 5 million learners impacted so far!
Creating sustainable social impact We work in close partnership with governments and multilateral organizations to operationalize skill-development and capability-building efforts as well as with companies in achieving sustainable impact through their CSR efforts. We are committed towards building a better tomorrow by enabling individuals, teams and organizations to fulfil their potential. Thus, building a better society and a better country.
