talent, business and social impact

Our new-age learning solutions, powered by best-in-class technology, Centum Learning, an ISO 9001:2015 and 27001:2013 certified company, is one of the most admired training solutions companies in India and Africa providing new-age learning solutions to accelerate business and impact livelihood. With over 3,000 learning and development specialists and their domain expertise in 21+ industry verticals, Centum Learning has trained more than 5 million people globally. Its mission is to build a future that is more productive and fulfilling for individuals and organizations. ...
With an extensive experience of over 15 years in talent development and management space, Centum Learning has provided customized end-to-end training solutions that have impacted the business of 500+ corporates in 15+ countries (India and Africa). Through its proprietary Centum 5D™ training methodology and use of its Learning eXperience Platform (LXP) – Centum LearnPro®, Centum is providing 360-degree learning solutions right from entry-level to mid-level. Be it sales and service academy, maximizing employee productivity, growing leaders from within, building winning teams, experiential training, ADC, bespoke content development solutions or LMS; Centum specializes in providing custom training solutions with a direct and measurable business impact.


Centum has been widely recognized across various respectable forums – it has received ‘Brandon Hall Gold Award for ‘Best Unique Sales Training Program’ 2021 and has been featured in the ‘2021 Training Outsourcing Watch List Companies’ worldwide. It has also been ranked #11 amongst India's Great Place to Work® in 2019.
