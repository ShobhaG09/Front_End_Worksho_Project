How we Work

Learning experiences are like a journey. It begins at the current state of the learner and ends where the learner is more successful. The end of the journey isn’t gaining more knowledge, it’s converting the knowledge into actions. Keeping this in mind, all our training interventions are focused on business outcomes.

Centum Foundation – Partnering for social impact

Organizations in the modern world, no longer work solely for profits. The focus on social responsibility has extended and it has shaped the organizational role and functioning towards the prevailing societal and environmental issues that require extended support. Centum Foundation* – the Corporate Social Responsibility company of Centum Learning, plays a pivotal role in helping design, monitor, and implement the CSR mandate of partner companies. Our CSR solutions are not merely aimed at delivering benefits but at creating a sustainable and self-running ecosystem that can bring meaningful change to lives and livelihoods.

To develop outcome-driven new-age learning solutions, the approach requires an entirely novel way of showcasing training excellence; an approach that changes the way training is conceived, designed, and executed over the long term. An approach that drives real behaviour change and results.

Curating Social Impact Programs

Corporates are increasingly seeking a diverse range of social impact programs across domains that are often aligned with their corporate values with an aim to bring out a positive change in society. They seek reliable CSR partnerships with trusted agencies that can serve as the ultimate guide around the best CSR practices for serving society! Centum aims to inspire collaborations among business leaders, community members, and a wide range of organizations to create a positive, significant and sustainable impact on society.
